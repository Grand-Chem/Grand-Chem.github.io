Advanced Blown Film Technology: Reifenhäuser EVO Excellence

How Grand-Chem's state-of-the-art Reifenhäuser EVO technology delivers superior packaging films with unmatched precision and quality for West African markets.

German Engineering Meets African Innovation

At Grand-Chem Limited, we've invested in the world's most advanced blown film technology: the Reifenhäuser EVO system. This German-engineered marvel represents the pinnacle of blown film manufacturing, allowing us to produce films that meet the most demanding specifications for food packaging, industrial applications, and specialty uses across Ghana and West Africa.

What Makes Reifenhäuser EVO Special?

  • Multi-layer Capability: Up to 9-layer film structures for superior barrier properties
  • Precision Control: ±2% thickness tolerance across the entire film width
  • Advanced Cooling: Enhanced bubble stability and optical properties
  • Energy Efficiency: 20% lower energy consumption than conventional systems
  • Quality Consistency: Automated control systems ensure every meter meets specification

Applications Across Industries

Our Reifenhäuser EVO produces food-grade films perfect for:

  • Snack food packaging with barrier properties
  • Fresh produce bags with controlled permeability
  • Dairy product overwraps with extended shelf life

Industrial Applications
