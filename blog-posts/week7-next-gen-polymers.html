Vivomer Technology: Marine-Biodegradable Innovation

Vivomer represents one of the most exciting developments in sustainable packaging materials. This revolutionary polymer technology offers unprecedented environmental benefits:

Vivomer Key Properties

Environmental Benefits

  • • Marine Biodegradable: Breaks down safely in ocean water
  • • Compostable: Industrial and home composting certified
  • • Non-toxic: No harmful microplastics released
  • • Carbon Negative: Net carbon removal during production

Performance Features

  • • Barrier Properties: Excellent moisture and oxygen barrier
  • • Heat Resistance: Suitable for hot-fill applications
  • • Clarity: Crystal clear transparency
  • • Printability: Compatible with existing printing systems

Bamboo-Based Polymer Revolution

Bamboo represents one of nature's most sustainable resources, and new polymer technologies are unlocking its potential for high-performance packaging:

Why Bamboo is Revolutionary

  • Rapid Growth: Harvest ready in 3-5 years vs 25+ years for trees
  • Carbon Capture: Absorbs 35% more CO2 than equivalent trees
  • Water Efficiency: Requires 30% less water than traditional crops
  • Soil Protection: Root system prevents erosion
  • Chemical-Free: Naturally pest resistant, no pesticides needed
